# Update Policy

## Static content

Update when major patches, reworks, or new content drops change canonical records.

## Systems content

Patch-sensitive mechanics should be reviewed whenever:

- damage formulas change
- status effects are reworked
- armor, shield, or overguard behavior changes
- mod stacking or interaction rules change

## Live content

Live rotations and world-state data should be treated as generated data sourced from import jobs rather than hand-authored records.
